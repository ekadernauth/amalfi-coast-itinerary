Procida
Procida Day Trip
  • Morning hydrofoil from Molo Beverello → Procida (~40 min)
  • Wander the pastel fishing village of Marina Corricella — impossibly photogenic
  • Walk up to Terra Murata — the medieval hilltop for sweeping views
  • Long seafood lunch at a harbour-side restaurant
  • Afternoon swim at Chiaiolella beach — calm, clear water
  • Hydrofoil back to Naples early evening
  • Easy dinner near the hotel — rest up
🚢 Only 40 min each way — much less time on water than Ischia

Arrive Sorrento · Settle In
  • Circumvesuviana train Naples → Sorrento (~70 min, easy)
  • Check in, lunch with a view of the Bay of Naples
  • Afternoon walk along the cliff-top path
  • Explore the flat historic centre — shops, ceramics, limoncello

Capri
Capri Day Trip
  • Ferry Sorrento → Capri (only 25 min — shortest crossing)
  • Morning in Anacapri — quieter, cooler, fewer crowds
  • Chairlift up Monte Solaro for the best panoramic views
  • Lunch in Capri town — people-watch at the piazzetta
  • Afternoon at the Gardens of Augustus — stunning cliff views
  • Ferry back to Sorrento late afternoon
⚠️ Skip the Blue Grotto boat — not suitable during pregnancy

Positano
Full Day in Positano
  • Morning ferry Sorrento → Positano (~35–40 min, scenic)
  • Pre-book sunbeds at a beach club — Lido Blu or La Scogliera
  • Morning swim at Spiaggia Grande
  • Slow lunch at a restaurant above the beach with views
  • Afternoon wander up through the village, terraces & boutiques
  • Aperitivo watching the golden light hit the buildings
  • Ferry back to Sorrento late afternoon/early evening
📍 7–8 hours gives you the full Positano experience
